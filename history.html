Lansdales History

This property began in the 1900s and is still tended by the original owner’s family. Alfred Buxton – a well-known landscape designer of the era influenced the garden with English trees, expansive lawns and ornamental lakes. You and your guests can enjoy champagne and canapés in the surrounds of 100-year-old trees and landscaped grounds.
